About this portfolio

The four sections here grew out of the assignments that structured EDUC 5390. Each began as a separate piece of work — a daily sketchbook habit, a lesson plan I tested with students, a small handmade book, an academic essay — but reading them side by side has shown me how much they speak to one another.

The portfolio is meant to be browsed slowly. The sketchbook opens with images and short captions; the lesson shares the plan and what happened when I taught it; the visual teaching philosophy is a book I made to hold beliefs I could not yet say in prose; and the culminating essay attempts to put those beliefs into the language of the field.
